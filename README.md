# 📊 Final Individual Project: ANOVA & Multiple Linear Regression on YRBS 2007

## 📖 Project Overview
This project analyzes the 2007 Youth Risk Behavior Survey (YRBS 2007) dataset to investigate a critical intersection of school safety and physical health. The core research question is: **"Is there a significant difference in BMI percentiles among high school students based on the frequency they were threatened or injured with a weapon on school property?"**

To ensure robust statistical conclusions, this project employs a two-step analytical pipeline:
1. **One-Way ANOVA:** To determine if mean BMI percentiles differ significantly across three threat frequency groups (0 times, 1 time, 2+ times).
2. **Multiple Linear Regression:** To test the robustness of the ANOVA findings by controlling for a key lifestyle variable (`AerobicExercise`), determining if the threat effect remains significant after accounting for exercise habits.
